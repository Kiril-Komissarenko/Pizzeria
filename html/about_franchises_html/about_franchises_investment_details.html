Investment Details

Slice Into Success with Project-K Pizzeria!

Project-K Pizzeria seeks an initial investment of $120,000 to cover essential startup costs, including leasing and renovating a 1,500-square-foot location in a high-traffic neighborhood, purchasing commercial kitchen equipment, and establishing an inviting dining area that comfortably seats up to 40 guests. Additional funds will be allocated to hiring and training skilled staff, implementing targeted marketing campaigns, and acquiring high-quality ingredients from local suppliers. This investment aims to position Project-K as a distinctive pizzeria offering both classic and innovative menu items, driving profitability through customer loyalty and strategic location advantage.
